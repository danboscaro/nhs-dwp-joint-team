Is it ok to carry on checking with your doctor that you can’t work, for as long as you’re unwell?

Your doctor will recommend how long you shouldn’t work for. This is sometimes called being “signed off”. If you’re still unwell after this time you need to go back and see them again.

Your doctor will confirm to us that you still shouldn’t be working.

If you don’t want us to confirm with your doctor that you’re still unwell you’ll need to send a copy of your sick note in the post.
