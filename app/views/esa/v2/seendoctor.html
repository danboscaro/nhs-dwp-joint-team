Have you been to see a doctor?

Did they say that you shouldn’t work?

Your doctor recommends how long you shouldn’t work for. This is sometimes called being “signed off”. If you’re still unwell after this time you need to go back and see them again.

Is it ok to confirm this with your doctor?

If you don’t want us to confirm with your doctor that you’re still unwell you’ll need to send a copy of your sick note in the post.

To get Employment and Support Allowance your doctor needs to confirm that you shouldn’t be working. If you think you’re too unwell to work, go back and see a doctor.
To make an application, you’ll need to see a doctor. The doctor needs to confirm that you shouldn’t be working.
